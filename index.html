What is Permaculture?

The Chicago Bike Guide was created by Steven Vance, a transportation planner and programmer in Chicago, Illinois, with the intention to carry a digital bike map without the need for an internet connection (some features may require an internet connection). All of the map tiles are stored on the iOS device.

Version

Latest app version is 0.8.3, September 20, 2013. Versions for data is under Attribution below.

Notes

  • Not all bike lanes marked in the map may be present or visible on the road because they've worn or the streets have been repaved recently. Some bike lanes exist only in one direction on a two-lane road; every attempt has been made to style these differently.
  • Train station markers most likely do not indicate the station entrances.
